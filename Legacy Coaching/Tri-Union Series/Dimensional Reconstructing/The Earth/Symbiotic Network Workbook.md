# SYMBIOTIC NETWORK WORKBOOK
### Companion Workbook to [[The Earth]] — The Art of Giving Back and Multiplying Through Connection

*The mycelial network does not ask for permission to exchange nutrients. It simply does.*

---

## What It Is

Installing mycelial logic into life and work. The 10% rule is not a suggestion — it is the minimum viable act that keeps the web alive and feeding back.

## Sections

1. **Mapping Your Current Web** — name the five people/entities most directly feeding your work; assess honestly whether you give back equal, more, or less than received; find where the exchange runs thin or one-sided.
2. **The 10% Practice** — choose this week's symbiotic act: to whom, in what form (money, time, introduction, knowledge), and the expected return to the web (not to you).
3. **Weekly Network Tending** (every Sunday) — who was fed this week, who fed you, where crab mentality showed up instead of tree logic, one adjustment for next week.

## The Vow of the Symbiotic Network

*I vow to stop extracting from my relationships and start circulating through them. I will perform at least one symbiotic act every week — giving ten percent or more back to the connections that sustain me — without keeping score. I trust the physics of the web. What I give returns multiplied.*
