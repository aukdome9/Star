## Tweet 1
The objection that always shows up: 'isn't calling yourself an anomaly or an old soul just spiritual narcissism?'

Depends entirely on what you do with the observation.

🧵

## Tweet 2
Narcissism uses it to create distance: I'm different, therefore better, therefore excused from your feedback. Compassion Over Power uses it to create responsibility: I'm different, therefore accountable for how I wield it.

## Tweet 3
The Hardware Problem is the actual check: software — insight, wisdom, frameworks — can't run on a nervous system that isn't ready yet. That includes yours. 'Anomaly' isn't an exemption from your own shadow work. It's an assignment.

## Tweet 4
New Category isn't a status symbol either — it's the honest description of not fitting existing boxes. Every genuinely new thing starts as one person the world had no vocabulary for, uncomfortable on purpose.

## Tweet 5
The actual test: are you patient with people earlier in their own development, or performing patience while quietly scoring the room? Old soul status was never permission to skip that question.

## Tweet 6

Not narcissism. The version of self-awareness that comes with a bill attached. Full doctrine: [[compassion-over-power-article]]. 👆

How does this change the way you move forward?
