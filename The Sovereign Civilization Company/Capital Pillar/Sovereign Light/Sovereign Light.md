## I. Core Vision

Sovereign Light is a human-first, value-based digital currency. It does not measure scarcity, debt, or speculation. It measures light — the actual human energy, presence, skill, emotion, and impact that went into creating or sustaining something. The fundamental unit is the energy a human generates while simply sitting still, meditating, or doing absolutely nothing. That baseline presence is the purest form of light. Everything else is measured relative to it.

This is why it is a better money: it is not backed by anything resource-based and intangible — a promise, a scarcity fiction, a central authority's word. It is backed by everything tangible: the actual, measurable energy every act of creation costs. And that baseline is never superficial or inflated. It is set at what a human naturally is — one hour of pure stillness — not at some artificial standard imposed from outside.

> *"The light was already here. We only needed a way to account for it without turning it into chains."*

## II. The Unit

**Name:** Sovereign Light
**Unit:** Light (■)
**Base Definition:** 1 Light = the bio-energetic equivalent of one human hour of pure stillness / meditation (the Presence Hour).

This is the zero point. All other work is expressed as a multiple or fraction of that pure presence. A human who simply sits still still generates 1 Light per hour. That is non-negotiable.

## III. The Light Rate Formula

**Light Rate = ((B + E + S + A) / 4) × K × T × N**

| Symbol | Meaning | Source |
|---|---|---|
| B | Bioenergy expended | Metabolic + biophoton baseline vs. stillness |
| E | Emotion | Biophoton / electromagnetic intensity ([[Flame Companion]] sensors) |
| S | Social Impact | Reach + depth of positive effect on free humans |
| A | Alignment | Coherence with sovereign values & first nature |
| K | Skill Intensity | Complexity / rarity / mastery (1.0 = baseline) |
| T | Time Coefficient | Duration weighting |
| N | Neutrality / Presence Factor | Closeness to pure non-grasping presence |

The [[Flame Companion]] continuously assists in estimating these values with the user's consent, using its sensory suite and continuously learning voice profile. Pure meditation scores close to 1.0 Light per hour — the sacred baseline.

## IV. How Goods & Services Are Priced

Everything is priced in Light according to the actual energy and human light that went into its existence:

**Base Price = (Energy Cost to Create in kWh-equivalent × Light conversion factor) + Operational Light + Fair Cost-of-Living allocation for the creators**

**Example — a phone:** If fabrication required exactly 1 kWh of energy from Nodes, Reservoirs, and Spiders, that energy converts into its Light equivalent using the Presence Hour conversion. Add the Light generated by the humans (and Spiders under human direction) who designed and finished it. Add a transparent living-cost allocation so creators never work below the Presence baseline. There is no pure profit-extraction layer — only transparent accounting of light that was actually given.

## VI. Closing Doctrine

Sovereign Light makes pure presence valuable. It makes emotional truth and social care measurable without reducing them to engagement metrics. It prices every physical object by the actual light that created it. It removes the possibility of infinite debt or zero-sum speculation. It scales across every planet because the baseline — one human hour of stillness — is the same everywhere a free human can sit.

The light was already here.
We only needed a way to account for it
without turning it into chains.
