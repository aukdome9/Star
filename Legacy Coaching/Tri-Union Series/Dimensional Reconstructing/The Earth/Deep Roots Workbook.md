# DEEP ROOTS WORKBOOK
### Companion Workbook to [[The Earth]] — The Practice of Mastery and Vast Knowledge

*The roots grow in the dark, in the honest places.*

---

## What It Is

Installation, not passive reading — moving Deep Roots from concept into an operating system, one domain at a time.

## Sections

1. **Naming Your Domains** — name the three domains most needing deeper roots right now, rate current depth 1–10 (be ruthless), name the excuse used to stay shallow in each.
2. **The Competitive Advantage of Depth** — identify someone operating from surface knowledge in your field, and what their work lacks that yours could have with more depth; name the cross-pollination that would give the largest advantage.
3. **Daily Root Review Practice** (21 days) — ten minutes, no phone: which domains were tended yesterday, where knowledge deepened versus stayed surface, one action to go deeper today.

## The Vow of Deep Roots

*I vow to treat mastery not as a destination but as the daily tending of my roots. I will no longer accept surface knowledge as sufficient. I will go one layer deeper in at least one domain every week, and I will let that depth cross-pollinate everything I build.*
