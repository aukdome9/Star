# Timeline

The Sovereign System Company runs Years 11–20 (~2037–2046) — the decade immediately after Legacy Coaching's own ten years (Years 1–10) come to a close. Ten years after Systems opens, the Sovereign Acquisition Company follows (Years 21–30); the Sovereign Civilization Company rises out of Acquisition's decade after that, roughly Year 31 (~2057).

A phased build-out within these ten years hasn't been set yet. Mission, process, and philosophy for this company live in [[The Sovereign Systems]].
