## How the Cadence Is Filled

Three supply sources feed the ~21 tweets/week target, shown transparently rather than papered over:

1. **New standalone tweets** — the 12 original Reality Architecting tweets (`the-dual-self-tweet-1..4`, `the-creator-tweet-1..4`, `the-field-tweet-1..4`) plus the 24 new cross-pollination tweets built this session (36 total new tweet notes).
2. **Thread-embedded tweets** — each thread (6 per thread) supplies additional individual tweet-length content within its own post; not separately scheduled, but part of the week's total volume.
3. **Recirculated real history** — the account's ~100 already-published Dimensional Walking posts (catalogued in [[Dimensional Walking]]'s Content Map) are legitimate, proven, high-signal material to requote/repost on days not otherwise filled. This is the literal mechanism of "boost our existing on-platform content."

Where the table below says **"draw from archive,"** that slot is intentionally left to be filled at scheduling time from the real post list in [[Dimensional Walking]] rather than inventing a new atomic file for it — the goal was volume with honesty about supply, not a padded-looking table.
