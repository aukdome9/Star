# SEED MAPPING WORKBOOK
### Companion Workbook to [[The Earth]] — Micro Fundamentals to Macro Vision

*Every great creation begins with a seed that contains the full code.*

---

## What It Is

Forces a creation down to its bare-bones fundamentals, then expands from there without losing the original intelligence. Most failed projects fail because the seed was never made pure.

## Sections

1. **Stripping to the Bare Bones** — state the absolute core of the current project in two sentences; describe it as if drawn on a napkin for a stranger; name any complexity added beyond what the fundamentals require.
2. **The Macro from the Micro** — name the three non-negotiable elements the creation must keep to stay true to its seed; describe what it would look like fully expressed while staying ruthlessly true to the micro.
3. **The Calm of Knowing** (Weekly Seed Check, every Friday) — can the fundamentals still be stated in two sentences; if not, what complexity crept in, and what will be stripped next week.

## The Vow of the Seed

*I vow to never again build on a seed I have not personally verified as pure. Before I invest significant time or resources in any creation, I will be able to state its fundamentals simply and draw its essence. I will let the macro emerge from the micro, not the other way around.*
