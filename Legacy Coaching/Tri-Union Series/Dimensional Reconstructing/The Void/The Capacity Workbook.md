# THE CAPACITY WORKBOOK
### Companion Workbook to [[The Void]] — The Empty Vessel Practice

*Nothing Owed · Nothing Owed Back · Right Capacity to Receive*

---

## What It Is

A mirror and practice ground for locating where debt — owed to you, or owed by you — is still being carried. Each honest answer expands the vessel; each capacity audit strengthens the muscle of receiving without negotiation.

## Sections

1. **The Debt Inventory** — list debts believed owed to you, and debts believed still owed by you to life, others, or your past self, without softening.
2. **The Three Capacities Audit** — rate mental, physical, and emotional capacity 1–10; name what would raise each by one point; identify the current bottleneck to receiving.
3. **Daily Receiving Practice** (7 days) — each evening: what was received without earning or explaining it, where justification crept in, what it would look like to receive that same thing from the Void.

## The Capacity Vow

*I am willing to let the debts dissolve. I am willing to become a vessel large enough and empty enough to receive what the field has always been prepared to give. I do not need to be owed. I do not need to owe. I only need to be here, honest, and available.*
