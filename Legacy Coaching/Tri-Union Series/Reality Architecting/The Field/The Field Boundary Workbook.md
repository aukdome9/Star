# THE FIELD BOUNDARY WORKBOOK
### Companion Workbook to [[The Field]] — The Practice of Sealing the Field

*You do not push the unwanted out. You become so precisely matched to the desired that the undesired can no longer find a home.*

---

## What It Is

Installation, not passive reading — moving the Field Boundary from an idea into a sealed, daily-enforced perimeter. The field does the holding once you have done the declaring.

## Sections

1. **Naming What's No Longer Allowed** — list plainly the people, dynamics, frequencies, patterns, sounds, and places no longer permitted in your field; do not soften the list to make it comfortable.
2. **The Incompatible Match Audit** — for each item named, identify where you are still an energetic match to it — the habit, the tolerance, the conversation that keeps a door open you claim is closed.
3. **Daily Field Sealing Practice** (21 days) — each day, stand as the center of your field, name what is no longer allowed, declare "This is incompatible with my existence. It cannot survive here," and track one line — where the field held, and where it leaked.

## The Vow of the Field

*I vow to stop negotiating with what no longer belongs in my field. I will declare my standard daily, until the field enforces it without my effort. I will become so precisely matched to what I am building that the rest simply cannot survive here.*
