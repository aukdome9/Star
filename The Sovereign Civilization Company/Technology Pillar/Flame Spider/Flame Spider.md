# Flame Spider

*Doctrine of the Self-Creating Fabricator*

The atomic unit of sovereign infrastructure. An eight-legged, self-replicating fabricator that converts iron and sunlight into more of itself and into the physical architecture of a free civilization — a closed metabolic loop, zero external mass after the seed generation.

Full doctrine: **[[Flame_Spider_Doctrine.pdf]]**

## Connections

- Powered, through nights and dust, by **[[Orbital Flame Nodes]]** via Tesla Beam.
- Prints the surface half of **[[Flame Lattice]]** — Hearths, rectennas, terminals.
- Its first seed on any new world is energized by **[[Energy Storage and Battery Integration|the Flame Reservoir]]** before local generation exists.
- Directed by **[[Flame Companion]]** — the Companion is the design authority and quality overseer for everything the Spider prints, including the Spider's own upgrades; the Spider is how the Companion's designs become physical.
- Reports the exact energy and directed light cost of every fabrication run into **[[Sovereign Light]]** — the price of anything a Spider prints is never hidden.
- Prints Father Nodes and the seed modules for Mother Nodes and Union Relays for the **[[Father-Mother Union]]** — the Tri-Union's hardware is Spider-made, same as everything else.
- Manufactures every train, vehicle, track section, charging station, and communal garage of **[[Flame Transit]]** — built inside the Hearts, same as everything else.
- Prints ASE clinics and Institution modules for **[[ASE Health]]**, school modules for **[[Flame Education]]**, and OAPA equipment for **[[Tri-Union Governance]]**.
