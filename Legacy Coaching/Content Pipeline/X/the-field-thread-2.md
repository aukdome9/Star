## Tweet 1
The real objection to Field work: 'isn't becoming incompatible with people just a nicer word for ghosting?'

No — and the difference matters.

🧵

## Tweet 2
Avoidance is subtraction by force: block, cut off, remove the trigger, hope it stays gone. It rarely does. Same pattern, new face, because the frequency that let it in never changed.

## Tweet 3
The Field is addition, not subtraction — you become so matched to what you actually want that the old pattern can't get traction, the way a smoker can't function somewhere smoking is structurally impossible.

## Tweet 4
Avoidance is something you do to someone else. The Field is something you become. One requires them to cooperate with your avoidance. The other doesn't require their cooperation at all.

## Tweet 5
That's also why it doesn't need re-doing every time the same type of person shows up. Avoidance is a decision repeated per person. A sealed Field is a standard, applied automatically.

## Tweet 6
