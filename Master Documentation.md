# Calendar & Content System

The temporal operating system connecting every transmission — X threads, TikTok clips, YouTube, newsletters, WHOP posts — to the exact day it's meant to land. `publish_date` in a content item's frontmatter is the single source of truth; the `[[YYYY-MM-DD]]` wikilink is the graph connection between the item and its date. Open any date and see everything transmitting that day.

- [[Content Hub]] — live dashboard: scheduled, in progress, ideas backlog, recently published.
- [[upcoming-scheduled]] — horizon view, everything ahead sorted by date.
- [[this-week-review]] — last 7 days posted, next 14 days scheduled.

**Structure**: `Content Pipeline/` holds every content item (`Active/`, `Archive/`, `Templates/`). `Calendar/Daily/` holds one note per day (`YYYY-MM-DD.md`), generated from `Calendar/templates/daily-note.md`. `Calendar/templates/insert-scheduled-date.md` is the Templater command (hotkey `Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+D`) that links a content item to its date — run it under a note's `## Scheduling` heading, then set `publish_date` to match.

Full setup, rationale, and failure-point mitigations: [[README]]
