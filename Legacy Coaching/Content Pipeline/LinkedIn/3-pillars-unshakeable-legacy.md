## Body
After coaching hundreds of reality architects, I've identified the 3 pillars that separate people who *talk* about greatness from people who *build* it:

**1. Sovereign Identity**
You cannot build a legacy on borrowed beliefs. The first pillar is knowing — not hoping, not wishing — *knowing* who you are and what you were designed to create.

**2. Systematic Execution**
Vision without a system is a dream journal. The second pillar is turning your clarity into daily, non-negotiable actions that compound over time.

**3. Community Architecture**
No legacy was ever built alone. The third pillar is surrounding yourself with people who sharpen your edge, not dull it.

These aren't abstract concepts. They're the exact framework I use with every client.
