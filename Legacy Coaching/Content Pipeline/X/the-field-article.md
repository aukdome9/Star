## Article Body

You do not push the unwanted out. You become so precisely matched to the desired that the undesired can no longer find a home.

Read that twice, because it's the opposite of how most people try to protect their peace. Most boundary work is subtraction by force — block them, cut them off, remove the trigger. That works for a while. Then the same pattern shows up wearing a different face, because you never changed the frequency that let it in. You just changed the address.

### The Incompatible Match

Principle III of Reality Architecting is the Field, and it starts from a different premise entirely: you are the field. Not a person standing inside a field trying to defend its edges — the field itself. Which means the boundaries that matter aren't walls. They're the frequency you emit.

Think about a smoker trying to survive somewhere smoking is illegal, fined, and structurally impossible to facilitate. Eventually they don't fight the rule — they just can't function there anymore. That's the model. Certain people, dynamics, sounds, and patterns become unable to survive in your space, not because you're guarding a gate, but because you've made yourself an incompatible match for them.

This is deeper than curating your environment or acting like the person you're becoming. It's the active, continuous declaration of what is no longer allowed — spoken through focus, attention, and standard — until the field enforces the boundary without you having to lift a finger.

### What the Research Actually Shows

Three separate research lines, three different methods, one converging answer.

**Line one — the street.** In 2008, Kees Keizer, Siegwart Lindenberg, and Linda Steg ran six field experiments in the Netherlands and published the results in *Science* under the title "The Spreading of Disorder." A single visible sign of disorder — graffiti on a wall, litter in a parking area — didn't stay contained. It roughly doubled the rate of unrelated norm violations nearby, including littering and even minor theft, in settings otherwise identical except for that one broken signal. Disorder gives permission for more disorder.
