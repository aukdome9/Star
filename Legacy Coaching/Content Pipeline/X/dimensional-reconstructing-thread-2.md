## Tweet 2
Flame without Void: forced manifestation, burnout, spiritual striving. Grasping the outcome instead of letting the field do what fields do.

## Tweet 3
Void without Flame: spiritual bypass, passive emptiness. A perfectly clean field that stays barren because nothing is generating the gravity to pull anything into it.

## Tweet 4
Earth without Flame or Void: grinding hustle, or empty network-building. Motion that looks like progress and produces very little that outlasts the motion.

## Tweet 5
All three together: the Flame generates gravitating spirit, the Void keeps the vessel empty enough to keep receiving, the Earth plants that spirit into soil nothing can topple. The doctrine was never partial by accident.
