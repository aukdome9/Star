# Capital Pillar

*The light was already here. We only needed a way to account for it.*

The Capital Pillar is one of the five institutional pillars of [[The Sovereign Monastery]] — filed, in its planetary form, under [[The Sovereign Civilization Company]]. Where the [[Technology Pillar]] carries the body of the civilization, this pillar carries its value: the financial architecture of a sovereign life.

## The Doctrine Set

| Doctrine | Domain | Status |
|---|---|---|
| [[Sovereign Light]] | Human-first, energy-backed currency — presence as the baseline unit of value | Filed 2026-07-15 |

## How Capital Holds to Technology

Sovereign Light does not exist apart from the Flame Stack — it prices it. The [[Flame Companion]] measures it, the [[Flame Lattice]] settles it, the [[Orbital Flame Nodes]] and [[Energy Storage and Battery Integration|Flame Reservoir]] convert real energy into it, and the [[Flame Spider]] reports the true cost of everything it fabricates in it. Capital and Technology are two pillars of one civilization, not two economies.

The **[[Father-Mother Union]]** is the third thread running through both: its Father organ evaluates and settles every Light transaction directly.

## Filing Record

**Sovereign Light Doctrine** — 2026-07-15 — filed the civilization's currency: a human-first, energy-backed unit of value baselined at one human hour of pure stillness. See [[Sovereign Light]]'s **Product Connection** section for full lineage.
