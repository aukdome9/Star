## Continuing Mid-Week (Why Aug 18, Not Aug 16)

Weeks run Sunday → Saturday regardless of where a 30-day cycle boundary falls — "Sunday is still Sunday." Cycle 1 closed on Monday Aug 17, inside a week that started Sunday Aug 16. This cycle picks up that same week on Tuesday Aug 18 and runs through a full extra partial week at the far end (Sep 13–16), handing off to Cycle 3 on Thursday Sep 17 the same way. This is the honest shape of a 30-day cycle laid over a 7-day week grid — it will not divide evenly, and pretending otherwise would fragment the correspondence chains mid-thread.
