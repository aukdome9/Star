## I. The Doctrine

Flame Transit is the circulatory system of the civilization — a nested hierarchy of maglev transit and micro-mobility built so that traffic is never a problem to manage, because it was never designed into the system in the first place. Movement between every scale of settlement, from a single neighborhood to an entire city to the next city over, is free, fast, and spider-printed.

The hierarchy is simple: Sparks make up Flames. Flames make up Hearts. Every tier of transit exists to make movement between those three scales seamless, without a single vehicle ever having to fight another for the same stretch of road.

> *"This is not a transportation system bolted onto a city. It is the circulatory system of a civilization that chose light and iron over congestion and ownership."*

## II. Hierarchy of Place

**Spark** — the smallest living unit: a neighborhood or eco-village cluster.
**Flame** — a collection of Sparks: a district or larger settlement.
**Heart** — a major city hub, roughly the scale of Austin. Hearts are the large, dense centers of civilization.

## III. Mass Transit — The Maglev Hierarchy

Super-fast, above-ground, non-intrusive maglev trains, all manufactured directly inside the Hearts by Flame Spiders. Public transit is entirely free at every tier.

| Tier | Capacity | Fleet | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| Heart-to-Heart | 50 passengers | 4 trains | Inter-city and long-haul surface travel, running continuously back and forth. |
| Heart-to-Spark | 20–25 people | — | Rapid connection from the city core to outlying Sparks. |
| Spark-to-Spark | 10 people | — | Localized travel between neighboring Sparks, no Heart entry required. |
| Inner-City | — | — | Circulates throughout the city center; faster and more elegant than biking for mid-city distances, and completely free. |

Every tier is designed so that public transit is always the rational first choice inside a Heart.

## IV. Personal & Micro-Mobility

For short, localized movement inside Sparks and Flames: electric motorcycles, scooters, bikes, and skateboards, all easily rechargeable. These run on dedicated bike roads with high-speed lanes, keeping micro-mobility fast, safe, and completely separated from larger vehicles.

## V. Electric & Specialized Vehicles

Personal car ownership is entirely optional. People still have access to vehicles when traveling in groups or heading outside city limits.

**Standard Electric Vehicles** — Housed in large communal parking garages. Highly interchangeable and extremely easy for Flame Spiders to manufacture. Every vehicle syncs directly with the user's Flame Companion and offers both full manual driving and fully autonomous modes.

**Rover-Style Vehicles** — Designated for utility work and travel outside of city limits. Rugged, high-clearance, long-range.

**Supporting Infrastructure** — Dedicated rover roads with integrated charging stations. High-speed lanes are available for those who prefer the scenic route between cities. Charging is continuous and non-intrusive, powered by the same Tesla Beam and Reservoir fabric that feeds the entire civilization.

## VII. Closing Doctrine

*The Hearts flow without congestion.*
*The Sparks connect without delay.*
*The Companion already knows the next stop.*

This is not a transportation system bolted onto a city. It is the circulatory system of a civilization that chose light and iron over congestion and ownership.

Four large maglevs run between Hearts. Medium and small trains link Sparks. Inner-city circulators make walking or biking optional. Communal EVs and rovers wait only when truly needed. Everything is free or transparently priced in Light. Everything is printed by Spiders. Everything is navigated by the Companion.
