**Ten-year roadmap for the Legacy Coaching Program — from initial certification to a decade of global public speaking.**

---

# Phase 1: Foundation and Book Releases (Years 1–2)

The initial phase focuses on establishing credentials and transitioning core educational content into a foundational book set.

## 1. Life Coaching Certification
Get certified as a life coach (targeted for six months from now — January 2027).

## 2. "The Tri-Union" Book Set Release
Originally planned as courses, these teachings are redirected to release as a three-book series:
- Dimensional Walking
- Dimensional Reconstructing
- Reality Architecting

---

# Phase 2: The Legacy Coaching Program (Years 2–3)

Once the books have been in circulation for a year or two, the active coaching program will launch with light promotion (approximately once a month) integrated into regular content.

## 1. Program Structure
A premium six-month program aimed at actively implementing the teachings of "The Tri-Union" within business and legacy creation.

## 2. Target Audience
Solo entrepreneurs and sovereign individuals looking to create legacy-changing or generational wealth.

## 3. Cohort Size and Networking
Limited to a maximum of eight people per cohort. This small group size is intentional, designed to serve as a tight-knit, founding starter network of fellow entrepreneurs to help members actively generate wealth within their communities.

## 4. Tailored Curriculum
The curriculum focuses on wealth generation, integration, and legacy creation. While covering these core areas, the syllabus will be customized based on:
- An initial interview process to identify specific individual needs.
- The active specializations of the cohort members.

---

# Phase 3: Global Public Speaking (Years 4–10)

Approximately two years after launching the coaching program, operations will expand to public-facing global events.

## 1. Tour Scale and Frequency
An annual tour traveling to 12 destinations around the world for open-admission public speaking events.

## 2. Core Themes and Teachings
Presentations will focus on:
- Legacy creation and the creation of a sovereign life.
- The importance of looking at oneself as a godly being.
- Heavy integration of the teachings, books, and series of William D. Watts.

---

# The Sovereign Monastery — The Capstone

Somewhere within these ten years, the Sovereign Monastery becomes real — not a metaphor, not a brand name, but an actual physical location. Its construction marks the culmination of this first decade: the point where everything Legacy Coaching built stops being taught and starts being a place.

Full doctrine for the physical Monastery lives in [[The Sovereign Monastery]].
