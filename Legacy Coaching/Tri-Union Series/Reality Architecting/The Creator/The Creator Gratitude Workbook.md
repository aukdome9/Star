# THE CREATOR GRATITUDE WORKBOOK
### Companion Workbook to [[The Creator]] — The Practice of Source Recognition

*Gratitude is not a request sent outward. It is a remembrance sent home.*

---

## What It Is

Installation, not passive reading — moving Creator Gratitude from a nice idea into the daily, felt mechanics of self-love. Recognition, practiced until self and Source stop reading as two different things.

## Sections

1. **Gratitude Inventory** — name what you are grateful for at the level of existence itself, not outcomes; notice where gratitude still feels transactional, conditional, or reserved for when things go well.
2. **The Separation Audit** — identify the places you still relate to the Creator, or to Source, as something outside yourself — waiting for permission, blessing, or proof before you claim what you already are; name the belief underneath each.
3. **Daily Gratitude Practice** (21 days) — each day, hands on the heart or open to the sky: declare "I am the Creator and the Creator is me," offer pure gratitude for the vessel and the moment, and track one line — where self-love and Source-love felt like one thing, and where they still felt like two.

## The Vow of the Creator

*I vow to stop treating gratitude as a request and start practicing it as a remembrance. I will love myself the way I love the force that made me, because they were never two things. I will let self-love become the boundary and the magnet it already is.*
