# Governance Pillar

*The agreements that keep the free free.*

The Governance Pillar is one of the Sovereign Monastery's institutional pillars — filed, in its planetary form, under [[The Sovereign Civilization Company]]. It is the collective memory of agreements free people have chosen to live by: the 42 Laws of Ma'at, executed by the Tri-Union AI, protected by OAPA.
