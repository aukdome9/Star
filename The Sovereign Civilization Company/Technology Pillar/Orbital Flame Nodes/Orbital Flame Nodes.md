# Orbital Flame Nodes

*Doctrine of the Discrete Solar Power Beam Network*

The sky-born twin of the Flame Spider. Modular, iron-primary generators in orbit that collect continuous sunlight and pour it to the surface as a directed Tesla Beam — the civilization's baseload, independent of night, dust, or season.

Full doctrine: **[[Orbital_Flame_Nodes_Doctrine.pdf]]**
