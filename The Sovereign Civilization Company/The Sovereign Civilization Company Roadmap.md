# Origin

The Sovereign Civilization Company does not found itself, and it isn't a sibling to the Sovereign Acquisition Company — it's what the Acquisition Company becomes. Legacy Coaching (Years 1–10) leads into the Sovereign System Company (Years 11–20), which leads into the Sovereign Acquisition Company (Years 21–30). Ten years spent finding, investing in, and acquiring the technology, resources, and companies that carry a sovereign future is what makes planet-to-planet installation possible — the Civilization Company rises out of that decade, roughly Year 31 (~2057), piggybacking directly on what Acquisition spent its ten years gathering.
