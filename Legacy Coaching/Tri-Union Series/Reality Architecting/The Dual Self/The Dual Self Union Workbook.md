# THE DUAL SELF UNION WORKBOOK
### Companion Workbook to [[The Dual Self]] — The Practice of Integrating the Shadow

*The version of you that has done everything wrong is not waiting to be punished. It is waiting to be welcomed.*

---

## What It Is

Installation, not passive reading — moving Dual Self Union from concept into the daily, embodied practice of ending the silent veto. Union is not a single breakthrough; it is repeated welcome.

## Sections

1. **Naming the Shadow** — name the version of you that believes it is inadequate, unworthy, or incapable of holding what you desire; describe plainly what it says when no one is listening, and rate how active its veto is right now (1–10, be honest).
2. **The Veto Audit** — identify three recent moments where you did not fail through confrontation but through quiet withdrawal — the missed follow-up, the shrunk opportunity, the sabotage dressed as a reasonable excuse; name which self made each choice.
3. **Daily Union Practice** (21 days) — each day, sit with the shadow for two minutes: see it fully, speak to it as a beloved child, feel its power return to the body. Track one line per day — what showed up, and whether it was met with acceptance or old resistance.

## The Vow of the Dual Self

*I vow to stop exiling the parts of myself that still believe they are unworthy. I will meet the shadow with acceptance, compassion, gratitude, love, and peace — never with war. I will call it home, again and again, until there is only one self left to claim what is rightfully mine.*
