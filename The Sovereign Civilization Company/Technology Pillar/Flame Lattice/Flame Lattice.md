# Flame Lattice

*Doctrine of the Unified Terrestrial Energy + Data Network*

One fabric, not two. Flame Hearths store power and route data from the same physical cell; a sparse constellation of dual-use satellites carries both to orbit and beyond. Energy and information travel the same lattice — the Solar Internet.

Full doctrine: **[[Flame_Lattice_Doctrine.pdf]]**

## Connections

- Its surface Hearths are printed by **[[Flame Spider]]**.
- Receives baseload power from **[[Orbital Flame Nodes]]** via Tesla Beam.
- Village- and city-scale Hearth Banks are the short-duration cousin of **[[Energy Storage and Battery Integration|the Flame Reservoir]]**, which is built for voyage-duration reserve instead of local buffering.
- Every **[[Flame Companion]]** authenticates to the nearest Hearth as its personal gateway and primary power source — the Lattice is how a Companion reaches the wider Roll and casts shared AR.
- Serves as the distributed ledger and mesh settlement layer for **[[Sovereign Light]]** — including long-haul and interplanetary settlement via its dual-use satellites.
- Hearths form the surface nervous system that feeds the Father organ of the **[[Father-Mother Union]]** in real time.
