# The Mandate

The Sovereign Systems Company exists to let individuals, governments, and businesses integrate and adapt custom, agentic AI and automated systems into their everyday lives. Every system we build is shaped to what a person or entity actually needs — established at the outset, through the interview process.

---

# The Interview Process

The interview process has one mission: identify every pain point stopping an entity from being efficient, productive, fluent, and easy to use.

## The Questions

- What is your job?
- What makes your job hard?
- What makes the way you do your job hard, looking at the tools and systems you currently use?
- What would make your job easier or make you more productive?
- If you had certain tools or software at your disposal, how would that allow you to be more productive in this space if we integrated it today?

These five questions are enough to see clearly into any entity we work with — individual, business, or government agency — and to integrate the future of technology into today's work.

# What We Build

Almost anything. A city, for instance, can hand us municipal payments, bus scheduling, and the rest of its local infrastructure, and we build the app that runs it.

---

# The Standard — Efficient and Sovereign

The point of every system we build is sovereignty, not just efficiency. A system that saves time but fights the person using it has failed. We do not build friction between a user and the thing meant to serve them — we build systems the individual can live inside authentically. That is the entire point of the Sovereign Systems Company.
