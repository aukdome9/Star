# The Eight Remembrances

*A working title — rename freely. This is a production tracker, not doctrine.*

Eight one-hour video sessions anchor [[The Value of the Human Experience]]. Each spawns its own clip set (TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts), podcast episode, subliminal track pair (1-hour + 5-minute), and 2–5 companion workbooks. No titles are locked — the eight below are proposed starting points, drawn directly from [[Value Content Doctrine]], for Aukdom to confirm, reorder, or replace.

- [ ] One-hour video (long-form)

- [ ] Podcast episode (audio cut of the full session)
- [ ] Subliminal track — 1-hour version
- [ ] Subliminal track — 5-minute version

## Notes

Sessions 5–7 are the most direct expression of the "Sources of the Signal" influences (Doctrine §V) — Dispenza, Napoleon Hill, and William D. Watts, respectively — though none should be presented as a lecture on the source thinker. Doctrine's own instruction holds: inhabit the teaching, don't explain the influence.

Once actual production begins, this page is the natural place to track real dates, `post_status`, and links out to each session's platform content, mirroring how [[30-Day Content Cycle — Cycle 1 (Jul 19–Aug 17)]] tracks Tri-Union's cadence.
