# 30-Day Content Cycle — Cycle 1 (Revised Cadence)

**Window:** 2026-07-19 → 2026-08-17 (30 days) · First cycle of the 90-Day Sprint, replanned under the new [[Posting Cadence]] contract.

**Cadence contract (full detail in [[Posting Cadence]]):** Sun–Sat week · **2 Articles/week** (Sun + Wed) · **4 Threads/week** (2 per Article, Correspondence principle) · **3 Tweets every day** (21/week), always calling back to a Thread.

**Quick links:** [[Content Hub]] · [[Posting Cadence]] · [[Legacy Coaching Calendar]] · [[n8n-manifest]] · [[Reality Architecting]] · [[Dimensional Walking]] · [[Dimensional Reconstructing]]

## What Changed From the Jul 20–Aug 16 Plan

The old contract was 1 Article + 3 Threads + ~3 tweets/day, anchored on Monday. The new contract doubles Articles to 2/week (Sun + Wed) and Threads to 4/week (2 per Article), and locks tweets to a strict 3-per-day, every day.

The good news: this isn't starting from zero. **The three Reality Architecting Articles were already dated on Sundays** — [[the-dual-self-article]] (Jul 19), [[the-creator-article]] (Jul 26), [[the-field-article]] (Aug 2) — a full week apart, untouched. That's the Sunday line of the new cadence, already built. The three Dimensional Walking/Reconstructing hub Articles ([[quantum-freedom-article]], [[compassion-over-power-article]], [[dimensional-reconstructing-article]]) only needed to shift **+2 days**, from their old Monday dates onto the new Wednesday slot. Their existing Threads shifted the same +2 days, from Tuesday onto Thursday, and now serve as each week's **Thread 1 (of 2)**.

What's genuinely new: a **Thread 2** for each of those six topics (12 Thread slots/week × 3 weeks of real inventory = 6 open slots — see Frontmatter/Backlog below), and the strict every-day tweet cadence.
